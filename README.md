
# Friz-BUFO
    A platformer inspired by minecraft's water bucket.

## Story:

You, Friz, have to help the UFO you found, BUFO, and collect the gem shard. It's currently incomplete, but there's enough to push!


## Controls:

- WASD and arrow keys to move

- W, up arrow, or space to jump

- left click to throw and retrieve BUFO



## Some helpful tips:

- the red pads on the ground are checkpoints, activated by BUFO's beam

- there are two gem shards to collect, one to the right in the mountain and one to the left amongst the towers
